🌱 Soil Preparation

Start with testing your soil pH and nutrient levels. Plow the land properly and add organic compost to improve soil fertility. Rotate crops yearly to keep the soil healthy.

💧 Watering Techniques

Use drip irrigation to conserve water and deliver it directly to roots. Water early in the morning or late in the evening to reduce evaporation. Always adjust your watering based on weather and crop needs.

🌾 Choosing Fertilizers

Prefer organic fertilizers like cow dung, compost, or vermicompost. Use NPK in balanced quantities based on crop type and growth stage. Over-fertilization can damage crops and pollute groundwater.

🐛 Pest & Disease Control

Implement Integrated Pest Management (IPM) — combine biological controls, crop rotation, and minimal use of pesticides. Use neem oil sprays as a natural insect repellent. Monitor your crops regularly for early signs of disease.
